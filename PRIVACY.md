# Privacy Policy for Arcile

**Last Updated:** 2026-03-17

Arcile is a personal project built with a privacy-first mindset. This policy explains how we handle your information.

## 1. No Data Collection
Arcile **does not collect, store, or transmit** any personal data, usage statistics, or telemetry from your device. 

## 2. Offline By Design
Arcile physically cannot send your data anywhere. The application explicitly omits the `android.permission.INTERNET` declaration, ensuring a hard technical guarantee that your files and activity remain entirely offline.

## 3. No Ads or Trackers
The app contains **zero advertisements** and **zero third-party trackers**. 

## 4. Local File Access
Arcile requires permission to access your device's storage (`MANAGE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE` or `READ/WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE`) solely to perform file management operations (copy, move, delete, etc.) as requested by you. This data **never leaves your device**.

## 5. Source-Available
Arcile's source code is publicly visible. You can audit the code yourself on [GitHub](https://github.com/qtremors/arcile).

## 6. Changes to This Policy
As Arcile is a personal project, this policy may be updated as new features are added. However, the core principle of **privacy, offline-only operations, and zero-data collection** will remain.
